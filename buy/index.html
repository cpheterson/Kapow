How It Works

1

Build Your Triads

Arrange cards into four groups of three sequential numbers. Lower is better — a 1-2-3 triad beats a 7-8-9.

2

Draw & Decide

Each turn, draw from the deck or grab from the discard pile. Place it, swap it, or toss it. Every choice matters.

3

Watch for KAPOW!

The wild card. It completes any triad — but it can also blow up your opponent's best-laid plans.

4

Go Out & Score

Complete all four triads to end the round. Everyone else totals their incomplete cards. Lowest score after 5 rounds wins.
